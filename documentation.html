anonConnect is a secure, anonymous social and messaging platform where users can interact, share multimedia content, participate in group chats, and play games, all while controlling their privacy. The platform supports both free and premium tiers, giving premium users additional privacy and engagement features.

Key Features:

  • Share and receive anonymous messages with images, text, audio, and video.
  • Premium features include identifying message senders and controlling who can view your details.
  • Group chat functionality for collaborative conversations.
  • Gaming section for interactive, anonymous play.
  • Complete anonymity for free users while offering advanced privacy controls for premium users.

Free Users

  • Send and receive messages anonymously.
  • Upload images, text, audio, and video.
  • Access group chats and play games anonymously.
  • Cannot see who sent messages.

Premium Users

  • See who sent messages.
  • Configure privacy settings to block certain users.
  • Full access to multimedia features and group chat settings.
  • Access additional gaming features and leaderboards.
  • Authentication required for personalized features.
  • Free users: minimal verification.
  • Premium users: account verification + payment.

Session Management:

  • Users remain logged in until manual logout or session expiry.
  • Unauthenticated users: limited content or restricted anonymous links.

Link Creation

  • Create unique links to share messages, images, audio, and video.
  • External sharing without revealing sender identity.
  • Premium: track clicks optionally.

Messaging

  • Free: fully anonymous.
  • Premium: see sender identity.
  • Supports text, images, audio, video.
  • Premium: delete or hide messages.

Privacy Controls

  • Premium: hide details from specific users.
  • Block users from messaging or viewing content.
  • All multimedia encrypted and stored securely.

Group Chat

  • Join/create groups.
  • Anonymous or attributed messages.
  • Admin moderation: add/remove users, restrict message types, manage visibility.

Gaming Section

  • Play interactive games anonymously or with friends.
  • Premium: track scores, see participants, access premium modes.
  • Game types: text-based, image-based, multiplayer.

Multimedia Support

  • Images: JPEG, PNG, GIF
  • Audio: MP3, WAV
  • Video: MP4, MOV
  • Files uploaded securely and linked via URLs.

Sign Up / Login

  • Create account with email/social login.
  • Verify account for premium.

Create a Link

  • Go to “Create Link” section.
  • Upload multimedia and generate link.

Send & Receive Messages

  • Open a link or group chat.
  • Send messages via text, audio, video, or images.

Group Chats

  • Chat anonymously or with username.

Play Games
